Who: This lesson assumes that you have practical knowledge of R and some prior exposure to RStudio. These requirements can be met by:

  1. completing a Carpentries Bioconductor Introduction to R Workshop
  2. independent exposure to R and RStudio

Additionally, this lesson assumes that learners are somewhat familiar with RNA transcription and high-throughput sequencing.

Profiles of hypothetical participants of the RNA-Seq workshop can be found on this page.

Participants should bring their own laptops and plan to participate actively.
